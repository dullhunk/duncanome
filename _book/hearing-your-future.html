5 Hearing Your Future

Hearing Your Future is a podcast which accompanies the Coding Your Future guidebook. We talk to graduands and graduates of computer science, see figure 5.1, to hear more about their journey from student to professional. As part of their exit interview we ask them what happens after graduation? What comes next?
